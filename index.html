Celia Swaney | WDD 130

Hi! My name is Celia Swaney and I am 21 years old, currently living in Provo, Utah! A few things I love to do are: hike, travel, play the guitar, go to concerts, surf, play tennis, and look at the stars. I was born in Bountiful, Utah and have moved nearly 10 times in my life. My favorite place I have lived was Kauai, Hawaii in high school! My best friend ever is my Savior Jesus Christ who has made me who I am today. Every single day He inspires me to have a kinder heart, a deeper mind, and a clear vision my future. He is the most important person to me, and even though I have a lot of fun hobbies, my time with Him beats all the others, because He is the root of my fulfillment. One year ago, I returned from serving God and His children in the Riverside, California mission. Those 18 months opened my soul to the power of heaven. Heaven truly is real and God really does know His children intimately and personally. My biggest hope for everyone around the world is that they will come to know Christ better, so they can experience the feeling of true Joy!
